Chicken Soup

Classic heart warming chicken soup for those cold winter nights. Great for a cold!

  • Chicken Breast
  • Chicken Stock
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  1. Cook Chicken breast fully or until white and juicy
  2. Bring chicken stock to a boil, then let simmer
  3. Cut up Carrot, celery, and onion
  4. Place all meat, veggies, and garlic into the broth
  5. Add in spices
  6. Let simmer for 6 hours
