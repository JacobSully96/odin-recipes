Ramen

A Japanese dish known all around the world, whether the cheap or expensive variety

  • Noodles
  • Chicken Stock
  • Pork Belly
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  1. Cook pork belly and cut into slices
  2. Saute ginger and garlic in a pan
  3. Add chicken stock and soy sauce
  4. Simmer liquid until aromatic
  5. Boil Noodles until soft
  6. Add noodles to the hot broth
  7. Serve in a bowl
