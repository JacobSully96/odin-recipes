Street Tacos

A classic Mexican dish that brings you to the heart of Mexico

A simple yet delicious dish. Savory, well seasoned, flat steak tacos that fit for any occasion

Ingredients

  • Flank Steak
  • Tortillas
  • White Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa
  • Seasonings / Marinade
    • Chili Powder
    • Soy Sauce
    • Cumin

Steps

  1. Cut flank steak into short 1/2 inch thick pieces
  2. Place meat into a zip lock bag along with the Seasonings, Soy Sauce and lime juice
  3. Let Marinate for a few hours
  4. Once marinated, cook steak in a skillet until meat and thoroughly cooked to your liking
  5. Cut up Onions, cilantro, and limes into wedges then set to the side
  6. Grab a tortilla and heat it up in a pan to make them warm and soft
  7. Assemble tacos with onion, cilantro, squeezed lime and salsa
